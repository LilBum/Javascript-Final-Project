About Plushy Co.

Welcome to Plushy Co., your one-stop-shop for the softest and cuddliest plush toys! We're a family-owned business that has been creating high-quality stuffed animals for over 20 years.

At Plushy Co., we believe that everyone deserves a little bit of softness in their life. That's why we're dedicated to creating plush toys that are not only adorable but also incredibly comfortable to snuggle with.
